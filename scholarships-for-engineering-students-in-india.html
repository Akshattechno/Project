14 Exclusive Scholarships for Engineering Students in India - 2024-25

Written by Sarthak Sharma on 30 April 2024

In India, it is estimated that over 13 lakh aspirants aspire to do Engineering every year after class 12th. But many of them need help and support to pursue a degree in their chosen college list.

Some lag behind due to fewer marks whereas most of them feel the financial burden of engineering fees on themselves and their parents which costs around 8-20 lakhs in India.

And that is why Scholarships for engineering students in India play an important role in funding the overall educational expenses and fulfilling their dreams. Central and state governments, numerous universities, private organizations, and corporations offer scholarships and fellowships to help these hopeful engineers pursue their desired advanced education.

Through this blog, let’s try to take a quick look at some of these scholarships on offer

Scholarships for engineering students by the Government of India

Central government focuses on the students of their country irrespective of their caste and background and helps them provide the best they can. They provide a range of scholarship programs to students who want to pursue Graduation or a Masters in engineering.

Annually, the government provides a substantial budget for education, with a significant portion allocated to scholarships to meritorious and financially needed students. Such of these scholarships are given below
